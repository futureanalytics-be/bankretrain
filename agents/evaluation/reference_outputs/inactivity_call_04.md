# Call Script — Re-Engagement Cashback Sprint (Call Follow-Up)

**Call type:** Outbound retention — re-engagement
**Agent context:** Starter account customer, 7-month tenure, very low app usage.

---

**Opening (mandatory opt-out — CH-002):**

"Hi, is that Rayan? ... Hi Rayan, this is Sofie calling from BankRetain. Quick thing before I go on — you can ask us to stop calling you about offers any time and I'll sort that straight away. Is it okay if I take a minute? ... Great.

I'm calling with a loyalty offer — not about any account issue."

---

**Pitch:**

"I wanted to reach out because we have a cashback offer available on your Starter account that I think you'll like.

Use your BankRetain card for five purchases in the next 30 days — that's five any purchases, no minimum spend, no specific shops — and we'll put €20 cashback directly into your account. It tracks automatically; you don't have to do anything except use your card.

It's just our way of saying thanks for being a BankRetain customer."

---

**If customer says 'I don't really use that account':**

"That's really useful to know — and the cashback offer is actually a good way to try using it for a few everyday purchases and see how it works for you. Even five small transactions — a coffee, a bus ticket — would qualify. No minimum spend."

---

**If customer has trouble with the app:**

"No problem at all. I can transfer you to our digital support team right now and they'll get you sorted in a few minutes. Or if you'd prefer, I can send you a link by SMS to the app download. Which would be easier?"

---

**Close:**

"The offer runs until 10 July 2026 — plenty of time. Is there anything else I can help you with? ... And if you'd rather we didn't call again, just say so and I'll update your preferences now."
