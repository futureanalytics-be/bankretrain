# Call Script — Tenure Appreciation Fee Refund

**Call type:** Outbound retention — loyalty gesture
**Agent context:** Private banking customer, 6-year tenure, 4 active products, NPS 5.

---

**Opening (mandatory opt-out — CH-002):**

"Good afternoon, may I speak with Ms. De Smedt? ... Hello, Ms. De Smedt, this is Laurent calling from BankRetain's private banking team. Before I continue, I want to make sure you know you can ask us to stop contacting you with offers at any point — just let me know and I'll update your preferences straight away. Are you happy to continue? ... Thank you.

This is a loyalty call — I'm calling to share something we'd like to do for you as a long-standing private banking customer."

---

**Pitch:**

"You've been with BankRetain for six years, and across that time you've built a relationship with us through four different products. We wanted to take a moment to recognise that.

As a tenure appreciation gesture, we'd like to refund your most recent annual card fee in full. That's processed by our team directly — you'll see it credited to your account within five business days, with no action required on your part.

I'm also here to check whether there's anything about your experience with us that we could be doing better. You're always welcome to share feedback directly with me — I'll make sure it reaches the right team."

---

**If customer raises a concern:**

"I'm glad you mentioned that. Let me make a note of that now and make sure a member of the private banking team follows up with you directly within two business days. You have my name — Laurent — and you can ask for me specifically if you call in."

---

**Close:**

"The fee refund will land in your account by the end of this week. Is there anything else I can help you with today? ... And again, if you'd prefer we don't reach out about future loyalty offers, just say the word."
