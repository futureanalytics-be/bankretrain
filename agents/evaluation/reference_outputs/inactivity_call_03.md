# Call Script — Five-Year Loyalty Milestone (Private Banking)

**Call type:** Outbound retention — loyalty milestone
**Agent context:** Private banking customer, 5-year tenure, moderately inactive.

---

**Opening (mandatory opt-out — CH-002):**

"Good afternoon, may I speak with Ms. Claessens? ... Good afternoon Ms. Claessens, this is Renaud calling from BankRetain's private banking team. Before I start, I want you to know that you can ask us to stop these calls at any time — just let me know. Is this a good moment? ... Thank you.

This is a loyalty call — I'm reaching out to mark something we think is worth acknowledging."

---

**Pitch:**

"This month marks five years since you opened your BankRetain account. Five years is a significant milestone, and we wanted to reach out personally to thank you.

As our five-year anniversary gesture, we'd like to credit a €50 loyalty reward to your account — with no conditions and no action required. It will appear within five business days.

We'd also like to invite you to a complimentary private banking review — a no-obligation conversation with one of our senior advisers to look at how your current products and services align with where you are today. It's entirely at your discretion and takes around 30 minutes.

Would that kind of review be of interest to you?"

---

**If customer declines the review:**

"Absolutely — no pressure at all. The €50 credit will still be applied regardless. If your situation changes and you'd like a review in future, you can always reach out to us directly."

---

**Close:**

"The credit is on its way. Thank you for five years with BankRetain, Ms. Claessens. Is there anything at all I can help with today? ... And if you'd prefer not to receive loyalty calls in future, please say and I'll update your preferences right away."
