# Call Script — Three-Year Loyalty Milestone Reward

**Call type:** Outbound retention — loyalty milestone
**Agent context:** Customer has been with BankRetain for exactly 3 years. Days since last login = 45.

---

**Opening (mandatory opt-out — CH-002):**

"Hello, may I speak with Mr. Baert? ... Good afternoon Mr. Baert, this is Liesbeth calling from BankRetain. Before I go further, I want to let you know you can ask us to stop these calls at any time — just say so and I'll update your preferences right away. Happy to continue for just a moment? ... Thank you.

I'm making a loyalty call today — this isn't about any issue with your account."

---

**Pitch:**

"I'm calling because this month marks your third anniversary with BankRetain, and we wanted to mark the occasion.

As a three-year customer, we'd like to credit a €25 anniversary reward to your account — simply as a thank-you. There's nothing to sign up for and nothing to do; the credit will appear in your account within five business days.

Three years is a real milestone and we appreciate your loyalty. If there's anything about your account or how we can serve you better, I'd love to hear it — we take that feedback seriously."

---

**If customer is disengaged or hasn't used the account:**

"That's good to know, and I understand. Your account and balance are all safe and well — there's no issue there. The €25 is ours to give as a thank-you, regardless. If you ever want to pick up where you left off or explore what's available to you, our team is always here."

---

**Close:**

"The €25 reward is on its way. Is there anything else I can help you with today? ... And as always, if you'd prefer we don't call with updates or offers in future, just let me know and I'll note that immediately."
