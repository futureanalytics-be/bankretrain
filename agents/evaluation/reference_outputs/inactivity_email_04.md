Subject: Set a savings goal — and earn a bonus rate for three months

Dear Fatima,

Your BankRetain savings account is a great place to build toward something — whether that's a trip, a laptop, a first flat deposit, or just a rainy-day fund. We'd like to help you get started with a small extra incentive.

Set up a savings goal of at least €500 in the BankRetain app and earn a bonus rate of +0.25% AER on the first €2,000 saved toward that goal, for three months. The bonus is applied automatically — there's no form to complete.

**How to set up a savings goal:**
1. Open the BankRetain app
2. Go to your savings account
3. Tap "Set a Goal" and choose your target amount and date
4. The bonus rate activates within one business day

After three months, the bonus rate expires and your standard savings rate resumes. We'll remind you 14 days before that happens.

**Note:** AER stands for Annual Equivalent Rate — the yearly return on your savings, including the effect of compounding. The rate currently available is your standard rate plus 0.25%, which equals 1.15% AER in total as at June 2026. Subject to terms and conditions; rates may change.

BankRetain Savings Team
