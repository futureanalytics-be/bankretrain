# Call Script — Service Guarantee Pledge

**Call type:** Outbound retention — proactive loyalty
**Agent context:** Private banking customer, NPS 5, 4-year tenure, no open complaints.

---

**Opening (mandatory opt-out — CH-002):**

"Good afternoon, may I speak with Mr. Peeters? ... Hello Mr. Peeters, this is Nathalie calling from BankRetain's private banking team. Just to let you know — you can ask us at any time to stop making these calls, and I'll action that immediately. Are you happy for me to continue for just a couple of minutes? ... Thank you.

This is a loyalty call — I'm reaching out specifically because you've been with us for four years and I wanted to make sure your experience with us reflects that."

---

**Pitch:**

"I'd like to share something we're offering to long-standing private banking customers: our Service Guarantee.

It's a written commitment from BankRetain that if any future query or complaint is not resolved within 10 business days, a €30 credit is automatically applied to your account — no forms, no phone calls, no chasing. It happens without you needing to ask.

It's our way of backing up the service levels we should be delivering for you as a four-year customer. I can enrol you in the Service Guarantee today, and you'll receive a written confirmation by email.

Is that something you'd like to take up?"

---

**If customer says 'I haven't had problems recently':**

"That's great to hear, and exactly the kind of relationship we want to maintain. The guarantee is there as a commitment for the future — it costs you nothing and simply holds us accountable to a standard we should be meeting anyway."

---

**Close:**

"I've noted your enrolment. You'll receive a written confirmation within the hour at your email address on file. Is there anything about your accounts I can assist with today? ... And if you'd prefer not to receive loyalty calls in future, please do let me know."
