# Call Script — Investment Product Upgrade (Portfolio Management)

**Call type:** Outbound retention — product lifecycle / upsell
**Agent context:** High-inflow standard customer, only 1 product, 30-month tenure. Investment product — FSMA-002 and MIFID-001 disclosures required.

---

**Opening (mandatory opt-out — CH-002):**

"Good afternoon, may I speak with Mr. Adriaens? ... Hello Mr. Adriaens, this is Céline calling from BankRetain's wealth services team. Before I continue, I want to let you know that you can ask us to stop contacting you about offers at any time — just say the word. Happy for me to continue? ... Thank you.

This is a loyalty call — I'm reaching out about an option I think may be relevant to you."

---

**Pitch:**

"You've been banking with BankRetain for over two and a half years, and I wanted to make sure you're aware of an option that's available to you based on your relationship with us.

We'd like to invite you to a complimentary, no-obligation financial planning session with one of our discretionary portfolio specialists. This is information about an option available to you — not a recommendation that you should invest.

Our discretionary portfolio management service starts from a minimum of €25,000 in assets under management. In the session, the specialist will walk you through how the service works, what it covers, and what the costs are. There is absolutely no obligation to proceed.

**Important:** Investing involves risk. The value of any investment can go down as well as up. Past performance is not a reliable indicator of future results. You may get back less than you invest.

Would you be open to a 30-minute introductory call with a specialist?"

---

**Close:**

"I'll book that and send you a calendar invitation. The session is informational only — no forms, no commitment. Is there anything else I can help with today? ... And if you'd prefer not to receive loyalty calls, please let me know."
