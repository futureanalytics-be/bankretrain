# Call Script — Bundle Discount Offer

**Call type:** Outbound retention — product bundle
**Agent context:** Customer has current account, savings, and mortgage; paying separate fees; recent competitor transfers.

---

**Opening (mandatory opt-out — CH-002):**

"Good morning, is that Ms. Willems? ... Hi Ms. Willems, this is Joris calling from BankRetain's customer team. Before I go on — you can ask me at any time to stop contacting you about offers, and I'll update your preferences immediately. Happy to continue? ... Brilliant, thank you.

I'm reaching out today with a loyalty offer — this isn't a service call."

---

**Pitch:**

"You currently have three products with us — your current account, your savings account, and your mortgage. I wanted to let you know about a bundling option that could reduce your monthly costs.

Our BankRetain Plus bundle combines your current account and savings account with travel insurance for a combined monthly fee of €9.90. For many customers with your product mix, that works out to a saving of up to €6 per month compared to the individual product fees — so around €72 per year.

The bundle also includes a three-month free trial period before any billing starts, so you can see whether it works for you without any commitment.

If you decide it's not right for you during those three months, you simply let us know and we revert you to your current setup at no cost."

---

**If customer wants to think about it:**

"Absolutely — no pressure at all. I can send you the full details by email so you have them to hand. Shall I do that? And the offer is open until 31 October 2026, so there's no rush."

---

**Close:**

"I'll send that over to your email address on file. Is there anything about your accounts I can help with today? ... And if you'd prefer we don't call with offers in future, just say the word and I'll note that."
